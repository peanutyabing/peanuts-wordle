HOW TO PLAY

Guess the word in 6 tries.
Each guess must be a valid 5-letter word. Hit "enter/return" to submit.
After each guess, the colour of the cells will change to show how close your guess was.
A new word will be available each day. If you are impatient, check the "unlimited" box to keep playing.
